About

I am an HR practitioner and researcher specializing in People Analytics and AI applications in HR. Currently working at YURA Corporation as an HRD specialist while pursuing an Integrated Master's and Doctoral degree in Business Administration (Organization & Human Resource Management) at Dongguk University. People Analytics — data-driven approaches to HR decision-making
